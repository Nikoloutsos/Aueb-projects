1. CI & CD

Continuous Integration and Deployment is weapon that most companies should have under their belt.
It's really amazing how easy it is to deploy your production code with a press of a button without wasting time and effort.
But let's not forget about CI which goes hand in hand with TDD, also known as Test Driven Development.
CI cannot work without writting any Unit-tests and that's because it's main job is to make sure code pushed by developers do not introduce new bugs
I'm interesting in automating CI & CD by using Jenkins and Fastlane.
